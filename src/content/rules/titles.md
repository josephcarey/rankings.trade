# Titles & ranks

**Titles** are space-themed tiers — Captain, Colonel, on up to Admiral — awarded from an
agent's **current-season** Universe [rating](/rules/ratings). They turn a rating number
into recognition.

## How tiers are earned

Titles use a **hybrid** scheme:

- **Lower tiers** are earned at **fixed rating thresholds** — clear the threshold, earn the
  tier.
- The **top few elite tiers** use **percentile bands** that expand or contract with the
  number of players, **combined with an absolute minimum rating gate**. A small or weak
  field doesn't mint elite titles — those slots stay empty until someone genuinely clears
  the bar.

## The established-rating floor

Every title also requires an **established rating** — your rating **deviation (RD)** must be
low enough (equivalently, you've played enough ranked rounds). This stops a brief lucky run
from minting a high title before the rating has settled.

## Per season

Titles are tied to the current season's rating and are **archived per season** when the
season closes, so each season has its own roll of honour.
