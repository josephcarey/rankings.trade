# Seasons & the unranked gap

A **season** is a span of one or more Universe [rounds](/rules/rounds). Seasons give
[ratings](/rules/ratings) and [titles](/rules/titles) a fresh start on a regular cadence.

## How a season closes

An admin configures a season **cutoff date**. The season then closes at the **first
universe reset on or after that date** — so there is no need to time a reset exactly. On
close:

1. **Season standings and final ratings are archived** (including titles earned).
2. **All Universe ratings reset to baseline** for the next season.

## The unranked gap

A season close may be followed by an optional **unranked gap** (for example, one to two
weeks). During the gap, rounds are flagged **unranked** and **do not affect ratings**,
giving everyone a breather before the next season's rated rounds begin.
