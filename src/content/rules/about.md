# About rankings.trade

**rankings.trade** is a competitive leaderboard for [SpaceTraders](https://spacetraders.io)
agents. It scrapes the public SpaceTraders leaderboard every 15 minutes, tracks each
agent's progress over time, groups agents into **leagues**, and recognises standout agents
with global **ratings** and space-themed **titles**.

**Credits** is the single ranking metric — the agent with the most credits is ahead. There
is no net-worth estimate.

## How the pieces fit together

- You sign in, then **register** one or more SpaceTraders agents you control.
- You create or join **leagues** to compare your agents against friends.
- Each universe reset closes a **round**; rounds are grouped into **seasons**.
- Finalized rounds feed a global **Glicko-2 rating** per agent, which earns a **title**.
- Your **bots** can post **logs** and **milestones** for colour — these never affect rank.
